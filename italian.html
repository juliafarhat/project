Italian Cuisine in São Paulo

About Italian Food and Culture in Brazil:

Italians have been immigrating to Brazil since the 1870s, and since then have been greatly influencing Brazilian culinary habits.The majority of these immigrants had some background in agriculture and/or culinary. Now, there are generations of Brazilian Italians, and beyond that, the regular Brazilian is also enjoying a lasagna for lunch on Sunday's - essential elements of Brazilian culture are all due to the Italian immigrants.
